OUTREACH // MISSION

Supporting the Community

We believe that no team should be held back by lack of hardware. To date, we have donated over $500 worth of VEX parts, including C-Channels, motors, and sensors, to three local rookie teams to help them get on the field.

STATUS: ACTIVE PROGRAM

The Power of PID: Inverted Pendulum

To demonstrate the precision required for high-level autonomous routines, we hosted a community event featuring our Inverted Pendulum Project. This project uses a custom PID loop to balance a vertical pole on a moving cart—a classic control theory challenge.

Watch the full demonstration on our YouTube channel.

Our Outreach Philosophy

Innovation isn't just about building the best robot; it's about raising the standard for the entire region. By sharing our code and donating our resources, we ensure a more competitive and inclusive robotics community.
