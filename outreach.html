Home Robot Competition Outreach

Beyond the Arena.

Building hardware is only half the mission. Building the community is the other.

The Power of PID.

We hosted a public workshop demonstrating the elegance of control theory through our custom-built Inverted Pendulum. By balancing a physical pole with pure logic, we showed local students the real-world impact of precise software engineering.

Resource Sharing.

We've donated over $500 in VEX components to rookie teams. From sensors to C-channels, we're ensuring that hardware limits never stifle a team's potential.
